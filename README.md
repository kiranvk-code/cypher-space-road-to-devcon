# Cypher Space ‚Äî Road to Devcon 8

**Ethereum Builder Series at NSS College of Engineering, Palakkad**

A proposal submitted to the [Ethereum Foundation University Program](https://esp.ethereum.foundation/applicants/rfp/rtd8_india_academic/apply) as part of the Road to Devcon 8 India initiative.

## About

Cypher Space is a student-led blockchain interest group under the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at NSS College of Engineering, Palakkad (Kerala, India). This repository contains our proposal, supporting materials, and evidence of community engagement for the Road to Devcon 8 program.

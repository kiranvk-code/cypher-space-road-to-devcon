# Road to Devcon 8: Ethereum Builder Series at NSSCE

## A Proposal Submitted to the Ethereum Foundation University Program

---

## 1. Project Overview

Cypher Space, the Blockchain Interest Group at NSS College of Engineering, Palakkad, proposes a comprehensive **Ethereum Builder Series** to take our student community from blockchain enthusiasts to actual builders. Building on our foundation of awareness sessions and theoretical workshops conducted over the past three months, this program will provide hands-on experience with smart contract development and DApp building — exactly the practical skills that the Ethereum ecosystem needs.

This proposal requests **$300 USD** in funding and **10 complimentary Devcon 8 tickets** to support our mission of bringing the next generation of Ethereum builders from Kerala to Mumbai in November 2026.

## 5. Proposed Program: Road to Devcon 8

### Program Timeline: July 2026

Our program spans the entire month of July 2026, leveraging the period when KTU's new semester (S3/S5/S7) is underway but before the first-year induction program begins in August (which occupies lab facilities). This window offers maximum student availability and optimal resource access.

### Phase 1: Reading Group & Foundation (July 1-10)

**Format:** Online + Seminar Hall (flexible)

**Activity:** Biweekly reading group sessions using our existing curated video curriculum. We'll focus on Phase 3 (Platforms & Applications) and Phase 4 (Deep-Dive Sessions) from our learning progression — specifically the smart contract and Ethereum development content that directly prepares students for the hands-on workshops.

**Sessions:**
- July 3: Smart Contracts Deep Dive (video + discussion)
- July 10: Ethereum Developer Environment (video + discussion)

**Deliverable:** Pre-workshop preparation checklist — each student completes assigned videos and arrives at the lab sessions with foundational knowledge.

### Phase 2: Hands-On Smart Contract Workshop (Week 3)

**Date:** July 15-16, 2026 (Two half-days)
**Format:** In-person, Computer Lab
**Duration:** 3-4 hours per session

**Session 1 — Solidity Fundamentals & First Contract**
- Introduction to Solidity syntax
- Writing a simple smart contract in Remix IDE
- Variables, functions, and visibility
- Deploying to Sepolia testnet
- Verifying on Etherscan

**Session 2 — Building Interactive Contracts**
- Working with mappings and arrays
- Creating a simple storage DApp
- Connecting contracts to a frontend (basic HTML/JS)
- Integrating with MetaMask
- Deploying and testing the complete flow

**Expected Outcome:** Each student deploys at least one smart contract to Sepolia testnet and demonstrates a working mini-DApp connected to their MetaMask wallet.

### Phase 3: Mini Hackathon & Showcase (Week 4)

**Date:** July 22-24, 2026
**Format:** In-person, Computer Lab + Online presentation

**Activity:** Teams of 3-4 students build a simple DApp applying what they learned. Possible projects include:
- Simple voting contract
- Basic token transfer application
- Document timestamp verification
- Simple marketplace prototype

**Showcase:** July 24 — Teams present their projects to faculty coordinators and invited guests. Best projects receive recognition and documentation for the EF report.
